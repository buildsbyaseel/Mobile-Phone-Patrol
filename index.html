LCD vs OLED — What's the Difference?

Budget Option

LCD Screen

A good quality replacement that works well for everyday use.

  • Lower cost option
  • Bright and clear display
  • Colors look good, but blacks are not as deep
  • Not identical to the original screen

Best if you want to save money.

OEM / Premium Option

OLED Screen

This is the same type of display technology used in the original phone screen.

  • Same quality as the original display
  • True blacks and richer colors
  • Higher contrast and sharper image
  • Feels like the original phone screen

Best for original quality and the best overall experience.
